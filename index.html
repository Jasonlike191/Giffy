Introduction to this Survey


This is a 2022 survey that provides song recommentation and/or a music playlist to add based on the evaluation of your personality and answer to the survey. Taking this survey might help you find a new artist you love, find a new favorite music and even understand your music taste better. Hope the song you get is one that you love!
